Term Project - Virtual Museum
Presented to Anthony Scavarelli
IMD3901 - Winter 2021

Welcome to Virtual Museum! An online experience where you can learn about the history of VR. Play solo or collaborate with other players to find the parts that compose broken exhibits and unlock their information sheet.

On desktop use the WASD arrows to move as well as the mouse to look around. On mobile, use the virtual joystick as well as touch functions to pan around.
